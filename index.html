Our Mission

Preserving Kasi Culture, Refining Taste.

At Kodijong, we believe the township's culinary heart is rich with untapped luxury. Our mission is to serve high quality Kotas, Burgers and home made sandwiches with love and care using local ingredients to provide authentic and delicious flavour to our patrons. We aim to be a convenient and welcoming food joint for takeaways, quick bites and sit-ins.

We focus on making our food available through various channels including whatsapp, call-ins and website ordering. KODIJONG however is situated in the classic area on the out skirts of Sandton, we also offer delivery services and yes at a cost depending on the distance. Our vision is to become the best food joint with efficiency that brings cultures, religions, ethnic groups and all ages in one roof and therefore we invite you to our stores today and be part of this big family.
